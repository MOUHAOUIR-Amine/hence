Outreach


HENCEFORTH was born with strong relationships with known experts and researchers from universities and research institutes at both national and international levels. To develop our R&D programs, we tie strong relationships with external research communities from all over the world. Our ongoing programs provide facilities that enable faculty and experts to collaborate with HENCEFORTH researchers and engineers. The involvement of international experts and researchers from all over the world, and of highly qualified engineers constitutes the necessary ingredients to carry out advanced research and to develop reliable software solutions that meet users' needs. To deepen our relationships with youths, HENCEFORTH offers scholarships, internships and other opportunities.
